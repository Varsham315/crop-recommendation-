Crop Information

Rice

Diseases: Blast, Bacterial blight

Benefits: High carbohydrate source, staple food.

Fertilizer Prevention: Use nitrogen-based fertilizers.

Maize

Diseases: Corn smut, Rust

Benefits: Rich in fiber, used in animal feed.

Fertilizer Prevention: Apply potassium-rich fertilizers.

Chickpea

Diseases: Fusarium wilt, Ascochyta blight

Benefits: High protein content, good for heart health.

Fertilizer Prevention: Use phosphorus-based fertilizers.

Kidneybeans

Diseases: Anthracnose, Root rot.

Benefits: Rich in fiber and protein.

Fertilizer Prevention: Use balanced NPK fertilizers.

Pigeonpeas

Diseases: Wilt, Sterility mosaic disease.

Benefits: High in protein, improves soil fertility.

Fertilizer Prevention: Use phosphorus and potassium fertilizers.

Mothbeans

Diseases: Yellow mosaic virus, Powdery mildew.

Benefits: Rich in protein and fiber.

Fertilizer Prevention: Use organic compost and balanced fertilizers.

Mungbean

Diseases: Cercospora leaf spot, Powdery mildew.

Benefits: High in protein and antioxidants.

Fertilizer Prevention: Use phosphorus and potassium fertilizers.
